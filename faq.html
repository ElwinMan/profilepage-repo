FAQ

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    You can print a document from your laptop by visiting http://print.hz.nl After authenticating, you can upload the file you would like to print. Optionally you can choose whether you want to print in color or not. Select the HZ Printer or HZ Plotter to request the print job. Now you can Register using your HZ Pass on the TouchID next to one of the printers. Select "Print / Afdrukken" in the menu and your document will be printed.

  2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    Find a printer near you and make sure you have at least €0.07 credit. Scanning is free but requires at least this amount of credit. Register using your HZ Pass on the TouchID next to the printer. Select "Scanning - Scan" in the menu. and lay your sheet on the feeder or glass plate Now press "Scan and Send", "Scan to me", "Yes" and hit the start button. Use the green button on the printer to scan page by page and press "Start Sending" when you're done.

  3. What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

    Stay home and tell your SCC that you are sick so that you can have online classes.

  4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    You can reserve a space from hz.nl if you are logged in and go to 'Selfservice Portal'

  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    First of all make sure to activate your HZ Pass for parking at the help desk. Free parking is available on the PZEM parking lot at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg.
